Pascal’s rule proof


We need to show

(nk)+(nk-1) = (n+1k)

Let us begin by writing the left-hand side as

n!k!⁢(n-k)!+n!(k-1)!⁢(n-(k-1))!

Getting a common denominator and simplifying, we have

n!k!⁢(n-k)!+n!(k-1)!⁢(n-k+1)! = (n-k+1)⁢n!(n-k+1)⁢k!⁢(n-k)!+k⁢n!k⁢(k-1)!⁢(n-k+1)!
= (n-k+1)⁢n!+k⁢n!k!⁢(n-k+1)!
= (n+1)⁢n!k!⁢((n+1)-k)!
= (n+1)!k!⁢((n+1)-k)!
= (n+1k)
