Theorem.

For any set of 8 integers, there exist at least two of them whose difference is divisible by 7.

Proof.

The residue classes modulo 7 are 0,1,2,3,4,5,6. We have seven and eight integers. So it must be the case that 2 integers fall on the same residue class, and therefore their difference will be divisible by 7. âˆŽ
