system of distinct representatives


Let S={S1,S2,…⁢Sn} be a finite collection of finite sets. A system of distinct representatives, or SDR, of S is a set

x1∈S1,x2∈S2,…⁢xn∈Sn

such that

xi≠xj⁢ whenever ⁢i≠j

(i.e., each choice must be unique).
