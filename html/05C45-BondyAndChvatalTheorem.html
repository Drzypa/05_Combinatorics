Bondy and Chvátal’s theorem.
Let G be a graph of order n≥3 and suppose that u and v are distinct non adjacent vertices such that deg⁡(u)+deg⁡(v)≥n.

Then G is Hamiltonian if and only if G+u⁢v is Hamiltonian.
